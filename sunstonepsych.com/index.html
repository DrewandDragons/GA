Work-Related Injuries are Tough...


Suffering from a work-related injury or illness can be deeply impactful. The many issues one experiences can be quite a lot to manage and often include an unclear course of medical treatment, ongoing symptoms, financial difficulties, undetermined ability to work, inability to engage in recreational activities, and confusion related to recovery or the claim in general. The experience is unique for each individual, causing a lot of stress that can be quite emotionally exhausting.


...We Are Here to Help


We are a highly skilled team of licensed mental health therapists that are located throughout Washington and surrounding states. Connecting with a therapist can be a great way to get the needed support in order to help the individual move forward in their personal and professional life. Telehealth and in-person treatment options are available.
